PROTECTING OUR PLANET START WITH YOU

"Bike more Drive less"

Bike commuting helps society in a number of ways. It's less wear and tear on the public roads, less pollution in the air, less traffic and is less noisy through neighborhoods.

"Volunteer"

A cleanup can bring about a sense of community and responsibility to keep our homes and common areas safe and clean.

"Reduce Reuse Recycle"

They prevents pollution, saves energy, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, saves money.

"Conserve Water"

The last water you use,the less runoff and wastewater that eventually end up the ocean

"Trash In Garbage Can"

Throwing trash all over the place will not only dirty our surroundings and pollute the environment, but also causes bad odours and encourages the infestation of cockroaches.

"Energy Efficient Light-Bulbs"

Energy efficient light bulbs reduce greenhouse gas emissions.Als flip the light swith off when you leave the home.

"Stop Using Plastic"

Buy less plastic and bring a reusable shopping bag.

"Stop Using Chemical"

Choose none toxic chemicals in home and office.

"Plant A Tree"

Trees provide food and oxygen.They help save energy,clean the air and help combat climate change.
